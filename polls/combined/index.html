Every pair of the 13 shared people plotted by how much they agree on sweets (X) vs how much they agree on powers (Y). Low scores = more agreement. Pairs in the upper-left agree on sweets but clash on powers; lower-right is the reverse.

📈 Correlation explorer

Pick any two item ranks — from either domain — and see how correlated they are across the 13 shared voters. Pearson r measures linear association; R² is the share of variance explained. With n=13 the bar for significance is high, so large r values matter most.
